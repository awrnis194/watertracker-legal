# Privacy Policy for Water Tracker

**Effective date:** April 17, 2026
**Last updated:** April 17, 2026

Water Tracker ("the App", "we", "us", "our") is an Android application that helps you log daily water intake and receive hydration reminders. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

By using Water Tracker you agree to the practices described below. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

---

## 1. Information we collect

### 1.1 Information you enter into the App
Water Tracker lets you log water intake and configure a daily hydration goal and reminder schedule. All of this information is stored **locally on your device** in the App's private storage. We do not upload, back up, or transmit your hydration history or goals to our servers — we do not operate any servers.

### 1.2 Information collected automatically by third-party services

**Google AdMob.** The App displays ads served by Google AdMob. Google may collect and process certain information in order to serve and measure ads, including:

- Your device's **Advertising ID** (a resettable identifier you control in your device settings)
- Approximate IP-based location (country/region level)
- Device information (model, OS version, language)
- General app usage information needed to deliver ads (e.g., which ad was shown)

Google's use of this data is governed by Google's own privacy policy: <https://policies.google.com/privacy>. You can learn about how Google uses data for advertising at <https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites>, and you can reset or opt out of personalized ads in your device's **Settings → Google → Ads**.

**Google Play services / crash diagnostics.** If you install the App via Google Play, Google may automatically collect crash logs, installation, and basic device metrics as described in Google Play's own policies. We only see aggregated, anonymized crash and performance reports in the Play Console.

### 1.3 Information we do NOT collect
We want to be explicit about what Water Tracker **does not** do:

- We do not require you to create an account.
- We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone input, or health records.
- We do not sell any information to data brokers.
- We do not track you across other apps or websites (beyond what Google AdMob does to serve ads, which you can disable as described above).

---

## 2. How we use information

- **Your locally stored hydration data** is used only to display your progress, compute streaks, and trigger the reminders you configured.
- **Advertising data processed by Google AdMob** is used by Google to deliver and measure ads inside the App, which is how we fund the App.
- **Crash diagnostics from Google Play** are used to identify and fix bugs.

We do not use your data for profiling, automated decision-making, or any purpose unrelated to operating the App.

---

## 3. Permissions the App requests

| Permission | Why the App needs it |
|---|---|
| `INTERNET`, `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE` | Required by Google AdMob to load ads. |
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` | To send the hydration reminders you opt into. |
| `SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM` | To deliver reminders at the times you schedule. |
| `RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED` | To re-register your reminder schedule after you reboot the device so reminders keep working. |
| `VIBRATE` | To vibrate the device (if enabled) when a reminder fires. |

You can revoke any permission at any time in your device's Settings. Revoking `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` will disable reminders; revoking `INTERNET` will disable ads (and the App will continue to work as a hydration tracker).

---

## 4. Children's privacy

Water Tracker is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the email below and we will take steps to delete it.

---

## 5. Data retention and deletion

- **On-device data.** Your hydration entries and settings live only in the App's private storage. They are deleted when you uninstall the App or use the App's "Clear data" option (if provided) or clear app data in your device settings.
- **AdMob data.** Retention of advertising data is controlled by Google and described in the Google policies linked above. You can reset your Advertising ID at any time in device settings.

Because we do not operate servers and do not collect personal information, there is no data we could retrieve, export, or delete on request. To delete all local data, uninstall the App.

---

## 6. Your rights

Depending on where you live (including the EEA, UK, California, and other jurisdictions with comprehensive privacy laws), you may have rights such as access, correction, deletion, portability, or objection to processing. Because Water Tracker does not collect personal information about you on our side, most of these rights are satisfied by controlling your device (uninstalling the App, resetting your Advertising ID, revoking permissions). For rights relating to data processed by Google AdMob, please contact Google using the resources linked above.

---

## 7. Security

We store your hydration data using Android's standard app-private storage, which is isolated from other apps on your device. Because we do not transmit this data off your device, it is not exposed to network-based attacks on our infrastructure. No security measure is perfect, however, and we encourage you to keep your device's OS and Google Play services up to date.
